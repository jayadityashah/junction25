[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:FINLAND] [CATEGORY:NATIONAL_LAWS] [PAGE:6]
PARAGRAPH_157242: This Act does not apply to financial activity pursued on an occasional or very limited basis when the following conditions are met:

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:FINLAND] [CATEGORY:NATIONAL_LAWS] [PAGE:6]
PARAGRAPH_157243: 1) the activity is limited;

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:FINLAND] [CATEGORY:NATIONAL_LAWS] [PAGE:6]
PARAGRAPH_157244: 2) the activity is not the principal business of the person but a directly related ancillary service accounting for no more than 5% of the turnover in the accounting period;

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:FINLAND] [CATEGORY:NATIONAL_LAWS] [PAGE:6]
PARAGRAPH_157245: 3) the activity is pursued by a person who does not engage in the activities referred to in section

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:FINLAND] [CATEGORY:NATIONAL_LAWS] [PAGE:6]
PARAGRAPH_157246: 2, subsection 1, paragraphs 1-23 as their principal business;

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:FINLAND] [CATEGORY:NATIONAL_LAWS] [PAGE:6]
PARAGRAPH_157247: 4) the activity is provided to the customers of the principal business and is not generally offered to the public;

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:FINLAND] [CATEGORY:NATIONAL_LAWS] [PAGE:6]
PARAGRAPH_157248: 5) the activity is not the money remittance referred to in section 1, subsection 2, paragraph 5 of the Act on Payment Institutions.
