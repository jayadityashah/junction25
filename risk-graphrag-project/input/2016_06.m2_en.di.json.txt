PARAGRAPH_30770: 4.1 Disclosure obligation as regards transactions in own shares

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:FIVA_MOK] [CATEGORY:FINANCIAL_REGULATION] [PAGE:8] [REQ_TYPE:MANDATORY]
PARAGRAPH_30771: (1) The issuer shall, in accordance with chapter 8, section 2, subsection 1 of the SMA, notify the operator of the regulated market on which its shares have been admitted to trading of transactions in its own shares. [JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:FIVA_MOK] [CATEGORY:FINANCIAL_REGULATION] [PAGE:8]
PARAGRAPH_30772: (2) By virtue of chapter 8, section 9 of the SMA, FIN-FSA hereby issues further regulations on the procedure to be applied by the stock exchange in regard to the disclosure of the issuer's transactions in own shares.

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:FIVA_MOK] [CATEGORY:FINANCIAL_REGULATION] [PAGE:8]
PARAGRAPH_30773: REGULATION (paragraphs 3-4)

PARAGRAPH_30774: (3) The stock exchange shall disclose the issuer's notification of transactions in own shares on its website without undue delay once the issuer has notified the stock exchange of such transactions in accordance with the rules of the stock exchange1. The stock exchange shall also submit such notifications to the Officially Appointed Mechanism (OAM) on a company-specific basis.

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:FIVA_MOK] [CATEGORY:FINANCIAL_REGULATION] [PAGE:8]
PARAGRAPH_30775: (4) If the issuer itself discloses its transactions in own shares, the stock exchange is not obliged to submit such notifications of transactions to the OAM.

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:FIVA_MOK] [CATEGORY:FINANCIAL_REGULATION] [PAGE:9]
PARAGRAPH_30785: 5.1 Public disclosure of regulated information

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:FIVA_MOK] [CATEGORY:FINANCIAL_REGULATION] [PAGE:9] [REQ_TYPE:MANDATORY]
PARAGRAPH_30786: (1) In accordance with chapter 10, section 3 of the SMA, the issuer shall disclose the regulated information in such manner as ensures fast and non-discriminatory access to the information. The issuer shall disseminate the information to the major media and make it available on its website.

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:FIVA_MOK] [CATEGORY:FINANCIAL_REGULATION] [PAGE:9] [REQ_TYPE:MANDATORY]
PARAGRAPH_30787: In addition, the issuer shall file the regulated information with the OAM designated by the Ministry of Finance, with the FIN-FSA and with the relevant regulated market operator.

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:FIVA_MOK] [CATEGORY:FINANCIAL_REGULATION] [PAGE:9] [REQ_TYPE:MANDATORY]
PARAGRAPH_30788: (2) In accordance with chapter 1, section 3, subsection 2 of the SMA, information that is revealed to be untruthful or misleading following its release and which may be of material significance to the investor shall, without delay, be appropriately corrected or supplemented.

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:FIVA_MOK] [CATEGORY:FINANCIAL_REGULATION] [PAGE:9]
PARAGRAPH_30789: (3) By virtue of chapter 10, section 9 of the SMA, FIN-FSA issues further regulations on the disclosure of regulated information.

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:FIVA_MOK] [CATEGORY:FINANCIAL_REGULATION] [PAGE:9]
PARAGRAPH_30790: REGULATION (paragraphs 4-8)

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:FIVA_MOK] [CATEGORY:FINANCIAL_REGULATION] [PAGE:9] [REQ_TYPE:MANDATORY]
PARAGRAPH_30791: (4) The regulated information shall be submitted to the media in full and unedited, which means that such submission is to be in a uniform file format, whatever its technical characteristics. The replacement of a part of the release by a hyperlink or attachment does not meet the requirement of disclosure in full and unedited.

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:FIVA_MOK] [CATEGORY:FINANCIAL_REGULATION] [PAGE:9] [REQ_TYPE:MANDATORY]
PARAGRAPH_30792: (5) In contrast to the provisions of paragraph 4 above, in the disclosure of periodic reports (half-year report (SMA 7:10 §), annual financial statements and management's report (SMA 7:5 § and Accounting Act 3:1 §) and corporate governance statement (SMA 7:7 §) it suffices to issue a release to the media containing the key information from these reports, together with a notice that the report has been disclosed. The release shall also indicate where the report is made available to investors. The release that is filed with the operator of the relevant regulated market operator and the OAM shall be accompanied by a full periodic disclosure report.

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:FIVA_MOK] [CATEGORY:FINANCIAL_REGULATION] [PAGE:9] [REQ_TYPE:MANDATORY]
PARAGRAPH_30793: (6) Regulated information shall be disseminated in such manner as to ensure efficient, fast and non-discriminatory access. The information channel for such disclosures shall be such as can be reasonably expected to disseminate the information effectively in Finland and the EEA area.

PARAGRAPH_30801: (7) The release issued for disclosure of regulated information shall clearly indicate the basic information, such as the name of the issuer, the release date and time, the subject of the release and a notification that it concerns regulated information.

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:FIVA_MOK] [CATEGORY:FINANCIAL_REGULATION] [PAGE:10] [REQ_TYPE:MANDATORY]
PARAGRAPH_30802: (8) Any errors or omissions in previously disclosed regulated information that may be of material significance to the investor shall be corrected.

PARAGRAPH_30803: 5.2 Availability of regulated information

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:FIVA_MOK] [CATEGORY:FINANCIAL_REGULATION] [PAGE:10] [REQ_TYPE:MANDATORY]
PARAGRAPH_30804: (9) In accordance with chapter 10, section 3, subsection 1 of the SMA, the issuer shall make regulated information available on its website.

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:FIVA_MOK] [CATEGORY:FINANCIAL_REGULATION] [PAGE:10] [REQ_TYPE:MANDATORY]
PARAGRAPH_30805: (10) In accordance with chapter 10, section 5 of the SMA, the issuer shall keep regulated information as referred to in chapters 7-9 and 11 of the SMA and information on the issuer's home member state selection as referred to in chapter 7, section 3, subsection 3 and section 3a, subsection 3 of the SMA available on its website for at least five years.

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:FIVA_MOK] [CATEGORY:FINANCIAL_REGULATION] [PAGE:10] [REQ_TYPE:MANDATORY]
PARAGRAPH_30806: By exception, the issuer shall keep the information referred to in chapter 7, sections 5, 7, 8 and 10 of the SMA available on its website for at least ten years.

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:FIVA_MOK] [CATEGORY:FINANCIAL_REGULATION] [PAGE:10]
PARAGRAPH_30807: (11) By virtue of chapter 10, section 9 of the SMA, FIN-FSA issues further regulations on the availability of regulated information.

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:FIVA_MOK] [CATEGORY:FINANCIAL_REGULATION] [PAGE:10]
PARAGRAPH_30808: REGULATION (paragraph 12)

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:FIVA_MOK] [CATEGORY:FINANCIAL_REGULATION] [PAGE:10] [REQ_TYPE:MANDATORY]
PARAGRAPH_30809: (12) The factual content of regulated information made available on the issuer's website shall not be changed afterwards.
