[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:LLL] [CATEGORY:FINANCIAL_REGULATION] [PAGE:1] [REQ_TYPE:MANDATORY]
PARAGRAPH_135800: A mortgage society shall mean a credit institution referred to in the Act on Credit Institutions (1607/1993). Its special purpose shall be to use funds mostly acquired as long-term loans to grant long-term loans mainly against a mortgage or other safeguard- ing collateral.

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:LLL] [CATEGORY:FINANCIAL_REGULATION] [PAGE:1] [REQ_TYPE:MANDATORY]
PARAGRAPH_135802: The trading name of a mortgage society shall always include the term "mortgage society".

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:LLL] [CATEGORY:FINANCIAL_REGULATION] [PAGE:1] [REQ_TYPE:MANDATORY]
PARAGRAPH_135803: Only a mortgage society shall be entitled to use the term "mortgage society" in its trading name or otherwise to indicate its activity.

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:LLL] [CATEGORY:FINANCIAL_REGULATION] [PAGE:1] [REQ_TYPE:MANDATORY]
PARAGRAPH_135804: A mortgage society shall be entitled separately to use a Finnish and Swedish trading name as well as, by permission of the Ministry of Finance, a trading name in another language.

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:LLL] [CATEGORY:FINANCIAL_REGULATION] [PAGE:1] [REQ_TYPE:MANDATORY]
PARAGRAPH_135806: The members of a mortgage society shall comprise its establishers for five years after its establishment as well as the parties taking out loans.

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:LLL] [CATEGORY:FINANCIAL_REGULATION] [PAGE:2] [REQ_TYPE:MANDATORY]
PARAGRAPH_135808: A party taking out a loan from the mortgage society granted out of State funds shall not be a member of the mortgage society.

[JURISDICTION:FINNISH] [SOURCE:LLL] [CATEGORY:FINANCIAL_REGULATION] [PAGE:2] [REQ_TYPE:MANDATORY]
PARAGRAPH_135809: A member of a mortgage society shall not be liable for the liabilities of the mort- gage society nor shall he be entitled to its property or to a share of any profit accrued.
