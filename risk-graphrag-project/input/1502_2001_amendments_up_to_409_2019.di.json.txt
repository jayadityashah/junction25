In other cases, the matter shall be transferred to be finally decided at a new Meeting of the Trustees to be held after one month at the earliest. An amendment to the bylaws which, at this meeting, is supported by at least two-thirds of the Trustees present at the meeting, shall be deemed approved by the Trustees.
